The Best Movies According to Billy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

This is one of the best sci-fi movies that has ever been created. Very beautiful film.

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse

Easily the best animated movies. The colors, the animations, the voice acting, the story is just perfect.

Amazing actors, great adaptation, the cinematic is very good.
